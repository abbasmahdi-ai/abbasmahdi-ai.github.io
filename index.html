Why this exists

Navigate AI confidently. Deploy it responsibly.

The EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, and ISO 42001 set a high bar for responsible AI governance. But the tools, training, and assessments that help organizations actually meet that bar are almost exclusively in English, built around Western regulatory defaults.

This toolkit bridges that gap: practical assessments built around PDPL, SDAIA, and SAMA, in Arabic and English, free and browser-based, with outputs you can act on the same day.
